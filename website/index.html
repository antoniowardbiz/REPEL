FAQ

Questions, answered

What kind of agency is REPEL?

REPEL is a marketing agency that works with creators. We focus on brand building, audience growth, social media management, and brand partnerships across mainstream platforms like YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Twitch, and more.

What types of creators do you work with?

We partner with creators across lifestyle, entertainment, gaming, education, music, fitness, and beyond. If you make content and want to grow it into a real brand and business, we'd love to talk.

Do I need a big following to apply?

No. We work with established creators and rising talent. What matters most is a clear voice, consistency, and a willingness to grow. We'll be honest about whether we're the right fit.

How does pricing work?

Every partnership is tailored to your goals and the scope of work. After a discovery call we'll send a clear proposal — no hidden fees, no long lock-in contracts.

Who owns my content and accounts?

You do. Always. We operate as your partner and team — your brand, your channels, and your content remain fully yours.

How soon will I see results?

Growth is a process, but most partners see meaningful momentum within the first 60–90 days. We report transparently every month so you always know what's working.

How do I get started?

Send us a message through the contact form below. We'll set up a discovery call, learn about your goals, and put together a custom plan.
